DevConf.in 2018 is the first annual Developers' Conference to be organised by Red Hat in Bengaluru, India. It is meant to provide a platform to the local FOSS community participants to come together and engage in the knowledge sharing through technical talks, workshops, panel discussions, hackathon and such activities.

When: August 4-5, 2018

Prostory: Christ University - Bengaluru, India

You should consider attending this DevConf event if you are:

  • Vývojář
  • Technology architect
  • IT konzultant
  • IT student nebo vyučující na IT univerzitě či fakultě
  • Nebo jednoduše nadšenec do IT, zajímající se o nejnovější trendy v open source a nově vznikajících digitálních technologií

DevConf events are free, without admission or ticket charge. On-line registration is required though.

Talks, presentations, workshops (etc) will all be in English.

The primary event themes this year are:

  • Agile
  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
  • Cryptography / Block Chain
  • Networking / Security Technologies / Research
  • Automation / Configuration Management/ Infrastructure
  • Cloud / Containers / DevOps
  • Community building / Diversity
  • Database / Storage
  • Developer tools / Debugging / Tracing
  • Design / UX
  • Desktop
  • Documentation
  • Hardware / Kernel / OS / Platform
  • IoT / Mobile
  • Middleware
  • QA Testing, Performance Optimization, Web Workload
  • Virtualization
  • Workshop
  • Serverless computing
  • Customer-Use cases
  • Messaging queues

Attendee Guidelines

All attendees are requested to follow DevConf.in Twitter handle (@devconf_in) and our facebook page (fb.com/devconf.IN) to folow the latest updates/announcements during the conference related to schedule change, session/workshop cancellation, speakers drop out etc in case of unexpected changes.

As it is monsoon time in India and we need to shuffle between two buildings it is advisable to carry an ☔ with you.

Attendees registration desk will be separate from speakers registration at the entrance of the Auditorium block. Please make sure you are registering at the right desk.

Check-in is managed using Eventbrite. Keep the email reply from Eventbrite that you were sent after registering. The QR Code you received is required on the day of the conference to complete check at the conference.

Registrations will start at 8AM. Please come early to avoid que. We expect the line will be long on day 1, so your patience and cooperation is appreciated!

Attendees are requested to save the DevConf.IN schedule page to their phone for the updates, the guide on how to get the page on your phone is as follows:

  1. Open this link on your browser, https://devconfin2018.sched.com/mobile/#page:page-schedule
  2. Then go to your menu of the browser and click on add to homescreen button.

The Christ university follows a strict 🚫 NO Carbonated/Aerated drinks 🚫 policy, so kindly refrain from carrying such drinks to the venue, also these will not be available in the campus.

Follow the code of conduct or you will be asked to leave the event: https://devconf.info/coc

All the sessions including keynote will take place in 2 buildings - Audi block and central block of the Christ University. Please refer the map below.

The Campus of Christ university is a specially abled friendly and wheel-chair accessible campus. ❤♿❤

All the sessions / workshops / Lunch space etc, will be available on first come first serve basis and limited access, so please make sure you arrive earlier for the sessions you are interested in. In case you don’t find seat, you should be able to attend a session in main auditorium of Cloud and Container track, which has a capacity of 2000 attendees.

Photography 📷 is allowed within the session and conference areas. In the common areas of the university like passages, internal roads and campuses, we request you refrain from photography.

No WIFI will be provided to any attendees except for workshops.

Please expect low connectivity in general at the campus and note there are no Mobile charging points available except the session rooms. Please carry a power bank with you to avoid power loss.

All attendees are requested to attend the opening and closing keynotes for both the days, as we are getting excellent speakers and it would be great if you respect their efforts by attending the keynotes.

Attendees can buy lunch / food from “Gourmet” at minus 1 floor in central block and Nandini Palace, opposite of central block.

DevConf Contributor Conferences are technology conference events for Linux and JBoss Developers, Admins, Linux and other open source project contributors, organized by Red Hat.

Our aim in organizing the conference is to provide opportunities for developers and contributors to meet and discuss future development of upstream projects and share updates and announcements about recent progress.

Red Hat is the leading industry provider of open-source software and operating systems. Red Hat's open-source software provides flexible alternatives to proprietary operating systems such as UNIX. Renowned within the industry, Red Hat employs more than 10,000 employees and maintains operations in 60 offices worldwide. More at redhat.com.
